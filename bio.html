Zekő Sebesy
Projects Bio Contact
Zekő Attila Sebesy (1997, Hungary) studied trumpet at the Musikhochschule Mannheim and at the Mozarteum University Salzburg. He is currently pursuing a degree in instrumental pedagogy as well as a master’s degree in wind orchestra conducting at the Mozarteum.
